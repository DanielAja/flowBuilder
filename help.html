Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Flow Builder

How do I create my first yoga flow?

Creating your first flow is easy! Simply click "Try Free" to access the Flow Builder. Browse through our library of 50+ yoga poses, drag and drop them into your sequence, and set the timing for each pose. Your flow will be automatically saved in your browser.

Is Flow Builder really free to use?

Yes! Flow Builder is completely free to use. You can create unlimited flows, access our full library of yoga poses, and save your sequences locally. We're working on premium features for the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Can I save and share my yoga flows?

Currently, flows are saved locally in your browser. You can export your flows and share them manually. We're working on cloud sync and sharing features that will be available in our Pro plan.

How many yoga poses are available?

Flow Builder includes over 50 yoga poses with detailed images and instructions. Our library covers everything from basic poses for beginners to advanced asanas for experienced practitioners.

Does Flow Builder work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Flow Builder is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can create flows on your computer and practice them on your mobile device.

Can I customize the timing for each pose?

Yes! You can set custom timing for each pose in your flow. The app will automatically calculate the total duration of your sequence and guide you through the practice with proper timing.

Is there a limit to how many flows I can create?

No limits! With the free version, you can create as many flows as you want. All flows are saved locally in your browser, so make sure to export important flows as backup.

What if I need help or have technical issues?

We're here to help! If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please reach out to us at daniel@flowbuilder.yoga. We typically respond within 24 hours.
